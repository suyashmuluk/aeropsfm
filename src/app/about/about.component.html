AEROPS Facility Management PVT. LTD. was established in India in 2021, and since then, we have rapidly evolved into a dynamic and forward-thinking organization. Our team comprises highly experienced Directors, proficient Team Leaders, and dedicated Members, all contributing their wealth of expertise to our operations. AEROPS stands as a burgeoning force in operations and maintenance services, specifically tailored for airports.
In line with our commitment to excellence, AEROPS has achieved key international quality and safety certifications. We are proudly certified with ISO 9001:2015 for Quality Management Systems, ISO 45001:2018 for Occupational Health & Safety Management, and ISO 14001:2015 for Environmental Management. These certifications reflect our unwavering dedication to delivering high-quality services, ensuring safe operational practices, and upholding responsible environmental standards in all our airport operations.

BCAS Certified Pioneers in Airport Operations and Maintenance

At AEROPS, we take pride in being recognized and certified by the Bureau of Civil Aviation Security (BCAS) authority, a testament to our unwavering commitment to upholding the highest standards of quality and security. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including expert installation and servicing of conveyor systems from major manufacturers, covering a wide expanse across India.
